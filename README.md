# FATES: The Ultimate Taxonomy of AI Agent Properties

Welcome to FATES Properties collection project.

## What's This About?

We're building **the definitive taxonomy of AI agent properties**. Think of it as a living, breathing map of every characteristic, behavior, and capability that defines how AI systems work and what they mean for humanity.

From trustworthiness to hallucinations, from calibration to honesty, FATES captures the entire spectrum of properties that matter when we talk about intelligent agents. It's an interconnected web of concepts, properties, and relationships that help us understand the DNA of AI systems.

## Why FATES?

Because we're tired of scattered discussions, conflicting terminology, and everyone reinventing taxonomy wheels. We need a **single source of truth**: a comprehensive, well-organized framework that the entire AI community can reference, build on, and evolve together.

FATES is:
- **Comprehensive** — covering everything from ethics to capabilities to failure modes
- **Interconnected** — showing how properties relate, influence, and depend on each other
- **Interactive** — visualize relationships in ways that make intuitive sense
- **Living** — constantly growing and improving with community input

## How to Use FATES

Open `index.html` in your browser and explore. Zoom, pan, hover over nodes to understand relationships. The taxonomy branches like a tree, where each node represents a crucial property of AI agents.
